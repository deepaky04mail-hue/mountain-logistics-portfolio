# Mountain Logistics Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes logistics operations across mountain regions using SQL and Google Looker Studio. The objective was to identify operational patterns, measure profitability, evaluate delivery performance, and present the findings through an interactive dashboard.

The dashboard allows users to filter results by mountain region, company, weather conditions, and logistics upgrades to understand how different operational factors affect business performance.

## Key Findings

### Finding 1: Spiti Region is Most Profitable
- Spiti generates ₹1.93M profit (58% of total)
- Only 20% of routes go through Spiti
- This means: We should send more shipments to Spiti

### Finding 2: June-August = Peak Season
- 71% of annual revenue comes in just 3 months
- This is risky because other months are slow
- Recommendation: Offer discounts in slow months to balance

### Finding 3: Logistics Upgrade = 15% More Profit
- With upgrade: ₹38,400 average profit per shipment
- Without upgrade: ₹33,200 average profit per shipment
- Recommendation: Use upgrades for all high-value routes

### Finding 4: Weather Affects Delays
- Snow causes 6-hour delays (worst)
- Clear weather causes 1.5-hour delays (best)
- Recommendation: Keep extra vehicles in snowy areas

### Finding 5: On-Time Delivery = Happy Customers
- On-time deliveries: 4.6/5 customer satisfaction
- Late deliveries: 3.8/5 customer satisfaction
- Recommendation: Focus on reliability to keep customers happy
