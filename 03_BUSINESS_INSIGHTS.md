# Business Insights

## 1. Regional Profitability

Among all regions, Spiti generated the highest total profit, while Uttarkashi recorded the lowest. This indicates that certain regions consistently contribute more to overall profitability and deserve greater operational focus.

---

## 2. Healthy Profit Margin

The overall profit margin remained around 71%, suggesting that operations are financially efficient with strong cost control across shipments.

---

## 3. Delivery Performance

More than 80% of shipments were delivered on time. Maintaining this performance can help improve customer satisfaction and long-term client retention.

---

## 4. Customer Satisfaction

Average customer satisfaction reached 4.55 out of 5, reflecting consistently positive delivery experiences.

---

## 5. Logistics Upgrade Impact

Shipments using logistics upgrades generally produced better financial outcomes compared to standard operations, indicating that operational improvements can positively influence profitability.

---

## 6. Weather Impact

Snowy weather showed the highest delivery delays, while clear weather experienced the lowest. Seasonal planning and route optimization can reduce delays during adverse weather conditions.

---

## Recommendations

- Allocate additional resources to high-performing regions.
- Continue investing in logistics upgrades where financially beneficial.
- Improve planning for snow-affected routes.
- Monitor customer satisfaction alongside delivery performance.
- Regularly review company-level profitability to identify top-performing partners.
